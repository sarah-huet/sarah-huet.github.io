Professional Experiences

Academic Research

Post-doc in Agri-food Economics 2023 – present
CESAER lab, INRAE, Institut Agro, France — Sup.: Valentin Bellassen
Modeling agricultural practices from accountancy data and public statistics and assessing their environmental footprint.
Using the Farm Accountancy Data Network and public statistics, developed a model to infer agricultural practices and estimate biodiversity impact, GHG emissions, water consumption and pollution at product and farm scale. Applied matching procedures on 5,500+ French farms comparing certified and non-certified productions. Contributed to an expert panel mandated by two French ministries.
PhD in Microbial Ecology 2019 – 2022
Agroecology dept., Univ. Bourgogne Franche-Comté — Sup.: L. Philippot, A. Spor, S. Romdhane
Deciphering biotic interactions and their role in soil microbial community assembly and functions.
Developed an innovative experimental approach with statistical techniques (linear, GLM, mixed models, multivariate analysis, network inference, multi-dataset integration) to causally infer effects of biotic interactions, spatial distancing, and community characteristics on microbial community assembly and functions.
